Ways to work with me

Four clear offers, explained simply

Band Night

A private-venue band experience for children who want to play with others, build confidence and work towards performing as a group.

School Band Programme

A small-group, all-electric school programme with a clear 6-week structure designed to get pupils playing together fast.

Make a Song

A creative course focused on songwriting, ideas, structure and turning a student’s own musical ideas into something real.

Private Lessons

Mobile one-to-one lessons in piano, singing, drums, guitar and bass, tailored to age, level and goals.

Tailored to the student or group

Whether it’s lessons at home, band-based learning or songwriting, everything is shaped around confidence, enjoyment and progress.

Flexible delivery

I can teach in homes, work with schools, and run small-group music sessions in suitable venues depending on the offer.

Safe & professional

Fully DBS checked and insured, with clear communication, safeguarding awareness and a calm, reassuring teaching style.

How it works

  1. 1. Tell me what you’re interested in
    Get in touch about private lessons, Band Night, Make a Song or the School Band Programme.
  2. 2. I recommend the best fit
    I can help you choose the right offer based on age, goals, confidence and whether it’s for one child, a friendship group or a school.
