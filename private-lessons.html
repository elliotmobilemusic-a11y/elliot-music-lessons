Lessons take place at home, helping families avoid extra travel and giving students a comfortable, familiar learning environment.

Common questions

When are lessons available?

Private lessons are usually available Wednesday to Friday. Final times are confirmed directly with Elliot depending on location and timetable.

What happens in a first lesson?

The first lesson is a chance to settle in, get to know the student’s starting point and begin building a plan that feels encouraging and realistic.

Do you travel outside Pudsey?

Yes, wider West Leeds and surrounding areas can be covered. Travel outside Pudsey may involve an extra fee depending on distance.

Can parents stay during lessons?

Yes — parents are welcome to remain on site and can stay involved as much or as little as feels helpful for the student.
