Policies & key terms

Everything important in one place at the bottom

Pricing sits first on this page, and the detailed policies sit here so families and schools can check the practical details when they need them.

Private lessons

I teach piano, singing, drums, guitar and bass in students’ homes, bringing the equipment needed for the lesson where appropriate.

Lesson fees

First lesson: £10. Standard lessons: 30 minutes £17.50, 60 minutes £32.50, 90 minutes £52.50.

Travel

Travel is added based on road distance from Pudsey and is shown clearly in the calculator above.

Cancellations

Please give at least 24 hours’ notice to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations with less than 24 hours’ notice may be charged at 50% of the lesson fee. If Elliot cancels, the lesson will be rescheduled at no extra cost.

Availability

Private lesson availability is currently Wednesday to Friday. Other times may occasionally be possible if confirmed directly.

Safeguarding & contact

Elliot is DBS checked, fully insured, and parents are welcome to sit in. Contact: 07368 975144 / elliotmobilemusic@gmail.com.

Band Night

£90 per child for a 6-week block, with a £10 deposit securing the place. Families commit to the full block.

Payments & missed sessions

Deposits are non-refundable unless Elliot cancels the block. Missed sessions are not refunded or rearranged because the session is planned for the whole band. If Elliot cancels, a replacement session or credit will be offered where possible.

Supervision

Parents must remain on site at all times. Sign-in and sign-out is required every week with a named adult, and no child leaves without being signed out.

Venue expectations

Band Night is typically delivered at Pirate Studios Leeds. Under-18s must attend with an adult account holder, and timings are arranged to fit venue rules.

School Band Programme

The parent-paid model is typically £15 per child per session, charged termly in advance. A standard 6-week block is normally £90 per child.

Group structure

Groups are usually 4–5 pupils, with a minimum of 3 pupils needed for a band to run. Sessions are typically 30 minutes weekly.

Payments & attendance

Payments are made upfront for the term. Missed sessions are not usually refunded. If Elliot cancels, a catch-up, replacement or credit is offered where possible.

School delivery

Timetabling, room use, pupil movement and supervision are confirmed with each school. Equipment is provided for in-school use.

Make a Song

The full 6-week course is £135, including songwriting lessons, recording / production lessons and the equipment / insurance fee.

Course structure

Weeks 1–3 focus on songwriting, melody, chords and structure. Weeks 4–6 focus on recording and production using Logic Pro X, microphones, an audio interface and MIDI keyboard setup.

Age & setup

Make a Song is for ages 6+. It can run 1:1 or as a group, but for group delivery all participants need to travel to the same house.

Outcome

Students leave with their own original song in progress or completed, along with creative confidence and experience using real studio equipment.
