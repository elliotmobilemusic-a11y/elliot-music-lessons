Common questions

Who is Band Night best for?

It is ideal for children who want to play with friends, enjoy group music-making and are excited by the idea of being part of a band.

How much does it cost?

The standard price is £90 per child for a 6-week block, with a £10 deposit used to secure the place.

Do parents need to stay?

Yes. Parents must remain on site at all times during Band Night sessions.

Can places be guaranteed?

Final places and groupings are confirmed directly by Elliot, so the best next step is to enquire through the contact page.
