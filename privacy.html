Privacy Notice

Last updated: 1 January 2026

Who we are

This website is operated by Elliot Wardill trading as Elliot’s Mobile Music (“we”, “us”).

Contact: elliotmobilemusic@gmail.com • 07368 975144

What personal data we collect

  • Contact details you provide (eg name, email, phone number).
  • Your enquiry details (eg lesson type, availability preferences, message content).
  • Technical information your browser shares when you use a website (eg IP address and basic request logs). This is typically handled by the services that host/deliver the site and forms.

Why we use your data (purpose)

  • To reply to enquiries and provide lesson information.
  • To arrange bookings, scheduling and lesson administration.
  • To keep basic records for business administration and safeguarding-related communications where relevant.

Lawful basis (UK GDPR)

  • Legitimate interests – responding to enquiries and running lessons as a small business.
  • Contract – if you book lessons, we use your details to deliver the service.
  • Legal obligations – where record-keeping is required (eg accounting/tax).

We don’t sell personal data and we don’t run advertising trackers on this site.

Who we share data with

We share data only when needed to operate the website and handle enquiries, for example:

  • Website hosting (eg GitHub Pages / similar hosting used for this site).
  • Form delivery provider (eg Formspree or similar, if used to send contact form messages).
  • Third-party content delivery networks used to load libraries (eg fonts/scripts), which may receive your IP address when your browser requests them.

How long we keep data

Enquiries are kept only as long as needed to respond and manage bookings, then deleted or archived when no longer required. If you become a client, some details may be kept longer for scheduling and business records (eg invoices) where required.

Your rights

You have rights under UK GDPR, including access, correction, deletion, and objection in certain circumstances. To exercise rights, email elliotmobilemusic@gmail.com.

You can also complain to the UK regulator: the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

Children’s data

Lessons are often for children, but this website is aimed at parents/carers. Please do not submit sensitive personal information about a child via the contact form. If needed for safeguarding/operational reasons, we’ll handle information carefully and only as required.
