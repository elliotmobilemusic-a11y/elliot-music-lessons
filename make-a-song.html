Creative project offer

Make a Song turns a musical idea into a finished original track.

Make a Song is a creative course for ages 6+ that helps children move from ideas to a finished piece of music. Across six weeks, students explore songwriting first, then recording and production, creating something that feels personal and exciting by the end of the course.

What this offer includes

Songwriting first

The early part of the course focuses on ideas, structure, lyrics and musical choices so the song has a strong foundation before recording begins.

Recording and production

The second half of the course focuses on recording and shaping the track using proper music equipment and production tools.

Creative confidence

The course is ideal for children who want something more project-based and imaginative than a standard weekly lesson.

Flexible format

It can run one-to-one, or as a group if all participants are able to travel to the same house for the sessions.

Common questions

Who is the course for?

Make a Song is for ages 6+ and suits children who enjoy creating, experimenting and building something original over several weeks.

Can it run as a group?

Yes — but for group delivery, everyone taking part would need to travel to the same house.

What is included in the price?

The course price is £135 and covers the six-week structure and equipment needed for delivery.

What does the student finish with?

By the end of the course, the aim is for the student to have created and shaped their own original song through the full process of writing and recording.
