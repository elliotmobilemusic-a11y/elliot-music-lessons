Cookies & similar technologies

Browsers can store small pieces of information on your device using cookies and similar technologies (including local storage and cached files). UK rules treat these similarly, and consent is needed for non-essential uses.

What this site stores (essential functionality)

  • Cookie notice choice – stored locally so we don’t repeatedly show the notice popup.
  • Contact form draft – the contact page can save your draft message locally to prevent losing it if you refresh.
  • PWA/service worker cache – if you install/use the app-like experience, your browser may cache files for performance/offline use.

You can remove local storage and site data using your browser settings.

Google Maps (travel calculator)

The travel calculator on the pricing page uses Google Maps to estimate route distance/time from Pudsey (LS28). When Google services load, Google may process technical data (eg IP address) and may set cookies depending on your browser settings.

How to control cookies / site data

  • Use your browser settings to block or delete cookies/site data for this website.
  • On most browsers you can remove “site data” (cookies + local storage) for a specific site.
  • You can also use the on-page Cookie settings button to re-open the notice.
