How the programme runs

  1. 1. School confirms interest
    We agree a day/time window and the room arrangements.

  3. 3. Bands are allocated
    Groups are set at 3–4 children per band with instrument balance in mind.

FAQ

For schools & parents
Does my child need experience?

No — this is beginner-friendly. Children learn simple band parts that work together.

What instruments are available?

Guitar, bass, keyboard, electronic drums, and singing. All-electric equipment is provided for sessions.

What if there aren’t enough children?

Minimum is 3. If numbers are too low, an alternative group, deferment, or refund may be offered depending on the situation.
