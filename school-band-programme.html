How the programme runs

Clear and simple

Step 1: Parents register and pay a £10 deposit.

Step 2: Bands are formed (minimum 4, maximum 5).

Step 3: Weekly timings confirmed with school once numbers are known.

Step 4: Remaining balance collected before Week 1 (deposit comes off the total).

Step 5: 6 weeks of sessions, ending in a mini performance.

Instrument options

Guitar • Bass • Keyboard • Electronic Drums • Singing

6-week timeline

  • • Week 1: Setup + first groove
  • • Week 2: Core parts + timing
  • • Week 3: Sections + transitions
  • • Week 4: Tighten band sound
  • • Week 5: Performance practice
  • • Week 6: Mini showcase

Pricing

What the school provides vs what Elliot provides

School provides

  • • A suitable room (quiet space if possible)
  • • A point of contact for timings / rota
  • • Announcement to parents + passing enquiries to Elliot

Elliot provides

  • • All-electric instruments + safe setup (cables / tape)
  • • Volume controlled (school-friendly)
  • • Parent communication + payments
  • • Teaching, planning, progress tracking

Safeguarding & safety

School procedure

Sessions take place on school premises and follow the school’s safeguarding expectations. Any concerns are reported via the school DSL / deputy DSL process.

Equipment safety

All-electric instruments with controlled volume. Cables are routed safely; if a cable crosses a walkway it is taped down.

Professional approach

Calm structure, clear expectations, and a supportive environment that builds confidence and teamwork.

FAQ

For schools & parents
Does my child need experience?

No — it’s beginner-friendly. Children learn simple parts that work together.

What instruments are available?

Guitar, bass, keyboard, electronic drums, and singing. All-electric equipment is provided for sessions.

What if there aren’t enough children?

Minimum is 3. If numbers are too low, an alternative option or refund can be offered.
