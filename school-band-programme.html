What the school provides

  • • A point of contact to confirm day/time

What I provide

  • • All-electric instruments + controlled volume
  • • Structured 6-week programme + end performance
  • • Parent registration + deposits handled online

Why it works

  • • Small groups (3–4) = calm & focused
  • • Band learning builds listening + teamwork
  • • Inclusive: children can start from scratch

Scheduling & groups

Bands are formed once registrations are in. The school and Elliot confirm a consistent weekly time. Minimum 3 children to run (max 4 per band).

Safeguarding

Sessions align with the school’s safeguarding procedures and take place on school premises. Any concerns are routed via the school DSL / deputy DSL process.

How it works (parents)

Skip to registration →

Step 1: Complete the form and pay the £10 deposit.

Step 2: Elliot confirms bands (3–4 children) and a consistent weekly time with the school.

Step 3: Remaining balance is collected once your child’s group is confirmed (before Week 1).

Step 4: Sessions run weekly (same day/time) and end with a small performance.

Deposit rules

  • • The £10 deposit comes off the total cost.
  • • If we can’t offer a suitable place/time, the deposit is refunded.
  • • If you cancel after a place/time is offered, the deposit may be non-refundable.
