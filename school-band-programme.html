6-week block timeline

Example structure
Week 1
Instrument intro, setup, first band groove.
Week 2
Core parts + steady timing.
Week 3
Song sections, transitions, confidence.
Week 4
Dynamics, listening, tighter band sound.
Week 5
Performance practice + polishing.
Week 6
Mini showcase / end-of-block performance.

What the school provides vs what Elliot provides

School provides

  • • A suitable room (quiet space if possible)
  • • A point of contact for timings / rota
  • • Announcement to parents + passing enquiries to Elliot

Elliot provides

  • • All-electric instruments + mixer + speakers (if needed later)
  • • Safe setup (cables/tape, volume controlled)
  • • Parent communication + payments
  • • Teaching, planning, progress tracking

Safeguarding & safety

School procedure

Sessions take place on school premises and follow the school’s safeguarding expectations. Any concerns are reported via the school DSL / deputy DSL process.

Room setup

Cables are routed safely. Any walkway crossings are taped down. Volume is controlled via mixer/levels.

Communication

Parents receive clear information about the programme, pricing, absences, and performance expectations.

FAQ

For schools & parents
Does my child need experience?

No — this is beginner-friendly. Children learn simple band parts that work together.

What instruments are available?

Guitar, bass, keyboard, electronic drums, and singing. All-electric equipment is provided for sessions.

What if there aren’t enough children?

Minimum is 3. If numbers are too low, an alternative group, deferment, or refund can be offered.
