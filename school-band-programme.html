All-electric kit

What equipment pupils use

The programme is designed to run smoothly in a school room without needing loud guitar amps. Everything is balanced through a mixer for clear, safe sound.

Core instruments

  • Electronic drums
  • 61-key keyboard + sustain pedal
  • Electric guitar + strap
  • Electric bass + strap
  • Wired vocal mic (also used for announcements)

Sound system

  • Mixer (control centre ‚Äî balance levels)
  • Powered speaker (PA)
  • Cables + power extension + safe cable management (gaffer tape)

Safety setup

  • Clear walkways, bags/coats out of the way
  • Cables taped down if crossing any walkway
  • Safe volume levels, following school guidance
  • Equipment never left unattended in corridors

Example pilot kit (typical models)

Exact models may vary, but the setup stays consistent.
