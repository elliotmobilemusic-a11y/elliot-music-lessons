School Band Programme

A structured, beginner-friendly in-school band programme for primary-age pupils: up to 4 pupils per band, 30 minutes weekly, and a short end-of-term showcase.

What pupils get

  • Weekly 30-minute band sessions at a fixed day/time
  • All-electric instruments provided: keyboard, guitar, bass & electronic drums
  • Beginner-focused learning with clear routines
  • One informal performance during the final session

Core rules

  • Groups are typically 4 pupils (minimum 3 to run)
  • Instrument choice is a preference — final allocation balances the band
  • Instrument stays the same for the full term block
  • Missed sessions aren’t refundable (the slot is reserved)

How a session runs (30 mins)

0–5 mins

Warm-up + rhythm + roles

5–15 mins

Instrument skill focus

15–25 mins

Band rehearsal / song building

25–30 mins

Run-through + goals + pack down

Term structure

  • Standard model: a 6-week block aligned to half-term
  • Week 0: sign-ups, payments, band allocation, schedule confirmation
  • Weeks 1–4: core learning + rehearsals
  • Week 5: performance prep
  • Week 6: showcase performance during the final session

Pricing (draft)

  • £15 per child, per session
  • Charged termly in advance (6 weeks = £90)
  • If Elliot’s Mobile Music cancels: a credit or replacement is offered where possible

Final details will be confirmed during pilot rollout.

For schools

  • Provided by Elliot’s Mobile Music: tutor, instruments, session structure, parent comms, payments, attendance tracking
  • Provided by the school: room/space, timetable support, and help getting pupils to/from sessions
  • Typical visit pattern: 3 bands back-to-back in one room
  • Safeguarding delivered in line with school policy and agreed supervision arrangements
